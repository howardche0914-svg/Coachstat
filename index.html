What is CoachStat?

CoachStat is a sports statistics and team management tool for basketball and soccer teams. It helps coaches track live game stats, player game time, team performance, and future AI video analysis support across phone, tablet, and laptop.

Team Management

Manage teams, rosters, player profiles, and game records in one place.

Reports

Review game summaries, player performance, and season trends.

Use future AI-assisted video review to catch missed plays and verify stats.
